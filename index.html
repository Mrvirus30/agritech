Fertilizer Dispensing Knapsack

AgriTech is a startup company that develops fertilizer dispensing knapsack that made up of common hardware materials that can be easily acquired and affordable. This knapsack offers advanced features, enhanced efficiency in applying fertilzer, and improved precision in the agricultural innovation. With a strong commitment to sustainability and maximizing crop yield, our startup aims to revolutionize the way farmers apply fertilizers, providing them with a cutting-edge solution that optimizes resource utilization and reduces environmental impact.
